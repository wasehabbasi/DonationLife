Donor Information

As a blood donor, you have the power to save lives. Your donation can help patients in critical conditions, including:

  • Accident victims with severe blood loss
  • Cancer patients undergoing chemotherapy
  • Patients with blood disorders like sickle cell anemia
  • People undergoing major surgeries

By keeping your profile up to date, we can match you with patients who need your specific blood type.

Eligibility Reminder

To be eligible to donate blood, you must:

  • Be between 18 and 65 years old
  • Weigh at least 50 kg
  • Be in good health
  • Have not donated blood in the last 8 weeks
  • Have no recent tattoos or piercings (within 6 months)
  • Have no disqualifying medical conditions
