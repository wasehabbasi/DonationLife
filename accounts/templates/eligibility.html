Eligibility Criteria

  • Age: 18 to 65 years old
  • Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Health: Good health with no infections
  • Time Between Donations: At least 8 weeks
  • No Recent Tattoos/Piercings: None in the last 6 months
  • No Disqualifying Conditions: Free from HIV, Hepatitis, etc.

Helpful Tips

Before Donation
  • Get a good night's sleep
  • Eat a healthy meal
  • Drink plenty of fluids
  • Avoid fatty foods
  • Bring ID and donor card if you have one
After Donation
  • Rest for 10-15 minutes
  • Drink extra fluids
  • Avoid strenuous activity for 24 hours
  • Don't smoke for 3 hours
  • Keep bandage on for several hours
